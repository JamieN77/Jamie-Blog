How do I Create a Post?

First you need to login by clicking the Login button at top-right corner of the home page.

If you don’t have an account you have two options:

  1. Register by clicking the Register link.
  2. Login with your Google account (you can set up your password later in your profile).

Once you are done registering you can use your email address and password to login. After successfully login click + button in the middle of the header:

You will see this page after clicking the button:

Now type in your article’s title and content! Note since this is a website about articles the minimum content length allowed is 300 characters. You may upload an image if you’d like. You are required to select one category and at least one tag for your article. Once you are finished click CREATE POST and your work is created!
